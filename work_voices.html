声を接ぐ Graft the Voices

2020- | Recording, Installation, Website (nh-voices.com)

────────────────

作者は2020年9月11日から毎日1回、自分の声を録音している。作者はこの行為を一生涯続行することを目指している。 身体と環境の変化と同一性を可聴化すると同時に、録音術の黎明期に濃厚だった、この技術に潜む「死」の匂いを取り戻す試み。（《A》から改題）

The artist has been recording his voice once a day since September 11, 2020. The artist aims to continue this act until his death. This is an attempt to make the changes and identities of the body and environment audible, and at the same time, to regain the scent of "death" hidden in this technology, which was prominent in the early days of recording. (Renamed from "A")
